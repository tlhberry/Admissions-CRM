2) Billing Rules
Trial

Every new facility starts trialing for 14 days automatically.

No payment method required during trial.

On trial end: lock only billing-gated features if needed, or show “Upgrade to continue” screen. Do not break access unexpectedly, but prevent further use until payment is added.

Seat counting

“Active users” = users with status active in the facility.

Billing recalculates seats at renewal (simple) OR immediately with proration (optional).

Keep v1 simple: bill seat count at renewal.

Charges

At renewal:

Amount = base fee + (active_user_count × per_user_fee)
